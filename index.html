Nan Wu is an Assistant Professor in the ECE department at the George Washington University. Her research interests lie in the joint area among computer architecture, electronic design automation (EDA), and machine learning (ML). She leads the research on ML for computer architecture and EDA, aiming to foster the virtuous cycle between hardware development and AI/ML. She received the Ph.D. degree in the ECE department at University of California, Santa Barbara, under the supervision of Dr. Yuan Xie, and the B.S degrees in Electronic Engineering and Economics from Tsinghua University in 2016.

We are always expecting self-motivated, talented, and hardworking students. Please contact me if you are interested in my work or pursuing a Ph.D degree.
Email: nan.wu@gwu.edu
